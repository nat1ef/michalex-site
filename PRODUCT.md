# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Tradespeople, factory maintenance staff, and individuals in Athens holding a broken or worn machine part (gear, shaft, coupling) or a drawing. They arrive from Google or word-of-mouth, usually on a phone, mid-problem. Their job: confirm within seconds that this shop can make/fix the part, that it is trustworthy, and how to call it.

## Product Purpose

Marketing site for Μηχανουργείο Αλεξανδράκης, a 40+ year machine shop at Καστοριάς 2, Athens (gears of every type, lathe and mill work, custom fabrication and repairs). The single conversion goal is a phone call to 210 522 2541; secondary is a Viber message with a photo of the part. Success = more calls from new customers.

## Brand Personality

Precise, seasoned, direct. "Premium industrial": the confidence of a specialist who has cut gears for four decades — modern and considered, never flashy or artistic. Feels like a well-lit, well-organized workshop, not a design studio and not a dusty brochure.

## Anti-references

- The previous dark cinematic scroll-video site (owner: "artistic, not professional") — no scroll-jacked storytelling, no dark theatrical theme.
- Flat 2005-era brochure sites (owner: "too plain") — no unstyled white boxes, thin gray text, default-looking sections.
- Generic SaaS landing patterns (gradient heroes, glass cards, identical icon-card grids).

## Design Principles

- Every screen answers "can they make my part?" and "how do I call them" without scrolling.
- Real workshop photography is the brand's strongest asset — imagery leads, decoration follows.
- Industrial texture (measurement marks, machined surfaces, technical labels) carries character; it must read as craft, not decoration.
- Depth and boldness over minimal flatness — but nothing that slows the page or hides the phone number.
- Greek-first copy, short sentences, a tradesman's directness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for all text. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for every animation. Tap targets ≥44px; the mobile call bar must never obscure content. Works on old Android phones on slow connections.
